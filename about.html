The bubbly and quietly admiring you!💖

My name is Crisa, and I am a student with a lot of questions about the universe.
Listing the best things that have happened in my life is something I love to do.
I want to be loved, admired, and become the best version of myself whether in good or bad times.
After graduating from senior high school, I lost my bubbly and joyful personality. I no longer felt the need or had the energy to talk to anyone.
I was burnt out, unsure of what to do, what to say, or even what to feel.
I felt lost in the world, among people, and in society.
I think that's one of the reasons I decided to take a break before entering college.
I wanted to find myself, to rediscover the old me the bubbly and joyful Crisa.
Yet, over time, instead of finding my old self, I found a new version of me.
I realized that I am no longer the same person. I am no longer the bubbly, joyful Crisa.
I became quiet for a long time and I learned to enjoy it. I found comfort in silence, in peace, in solitude.
I embraced the new me, the Crisa who is both bubbly and quietly admiring you.
This time around, I want to be free and do what I truly want. I don't want to be controlled anymore.
Yet, I am still a Swiftie and a lover of the universe!
